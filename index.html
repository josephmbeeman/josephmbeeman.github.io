Executive Profile

Builder. Executive.
Entrepreneur. Strategist.

20 years leading capital projects from feasibility through delivery across commercial, energy, federal, and infrastructure sectors. I operate at the intersection of capital strategy, development, and construction execution — aligning underwriting assumptions, project scope, and delivery to protect investment outcomes.

Current Focus Areas

  • Data center pre-development consulting ($50M–$3.5B projected)
  • DOE mission-critical infrastructure, NNSS
  • Capital project controls & cost strategy
  • AI-enhanced pro forma & Monte Carlo risk models
  • MPS Real Estate Finance — Georgetown University

As Director at Lawrence Construction, I took a pile-driving-only operation doing $200K in revenue and built it into a nearly $20M division by expanding scope into CIP structural concrete, drilled shafts, temporary shoring, tieback installation, and multi-crew pile operations — while also expanding geographically into Utah, Wyoming, Nebraska, and Iowa, and pursuing large-scale solar project work. All at 20–30% margins with a team that grew from 5 to 40+ staff. By year four we were bidding over $1.5B in work annually.

Through Beeman Construction Inc. / Beeman Consulting, I've served PE-backed developers, family offices, and federal agencies on programs from $10M to $500M+. Currently consulting on data center pre-development — supporting feasibility, financial modeling, and capital structuring for projects from $50M to $3.5B in pipeline value.

Completing an MPS in Real Estate Finance & Development at Georgetown University, layering graduate-level capital markets expertise on top of two decades of field operations. This combination — deep execution knowledge merged with financial sophistication — enables me to translate between engineers, operators, developers, and capital providers.
