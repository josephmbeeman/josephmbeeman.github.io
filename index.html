Executive Profile

Builder. Operator.
Strategist. Developer.

Over two decades leading capital projects across commercial development, energy infrastructure, federal facilities, and advanced engineering. I operate at the convergence of capital strategy, development execution, and construction management — aligning underwriting assumptions with project scope and delivery timelines to protect and enhance investment outcomes.
